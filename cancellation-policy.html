Cancellation Policy

Rules regarding booking cancellations

We understand that plans can change. If you need to cancel your booking, please review our cancellation tiers below. Cancellation requests must be sent via email to booking@safariwithakash.com.

Cancellation Charges

Time Before Safari Date Refund Amount
More than 30 Days 75% of Safari Cost
15 to 30 Days 50% of Safari Cost
Less than 15 Days No Refund

Important Note:

The Government Safari Permit Fee component of your booking is strictly NON-REFUNDABLE under any circumstances once the permit is issued by the Forest Department. The refund percentages above apply only to the service charges and vehicle/guide fees.

No Show Policy

If the guest fails to show up for the safari at the designated pickup time or entry gate, it will be treated as a "No Show," and no refund will be provided.
