Terms & Conditions

Please read carefully before booking

1. Booking Policy

  • Safari With Akash (Reserve India Tours) acts as a booking agent for Panna National Park.
  • Safari booking is subject to availability of permits issued by the Forest Department.
  • All bookings must be confirmed with a 50% to 100% advance payment depending on the season.
  • Safari zones are allotted by the Forest Department and cannot be changed by us.

2. Mandatory ID Proof

All guests must carry the same original ID proof (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport, or Driving License) that was used during the booking. If the details do not match at the entry gate, the park authorities may deny entry, and no refund will be provided.

3. Guest Conduct

Guests must adhere to the rules of Panna National Park. Getting down from the vehicle, shouting, littering, or feeding animals is strictly prohibited. Any violation may lead to immediate termination of the safari without refund.

4. Liability

Wildlife safaris involve inherent risks. While we take utmost care for safety, Safari With Akash or its agents are not responsible for any accident, injury, theft, or loss of baggage during the tour. Guests travel at their own risk.

5. Jurisdiction

Any disputes arising out of these terms and conditions shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Panna, Madhya Pradesh, India.
