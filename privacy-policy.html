Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

At Safari With Akash (operated by Reserve India Tours), accessible from safariwithakash.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Safari With Akash and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The information we collect typically includes:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, nationality, and age.
  • Identity Proof: Details from Passports, Aadhaar Cards, or Voter IDs required strictly for Forest Department permits and bookings.
  • Booking Details: Travel dates, safari preferences, and accommodation requirements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Process your safari and hotel bookings with the Panna National Park authorities.
  • Communicate with you regarding your booking status, changes, or updates.
  • Send you emails regarding safety guidelines or itinerary details.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website to improve user experience.

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your ID proofs are submitted directly to the Forest Department for permit generation and are not shared with any unauthorized third parties. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to outside parties.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., payment gateways or government portals). We have no control over the content and privacy policies of those sites and cannot accept responsibility or liability for their respective policies.

5. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at booking@safariwithakash.com.
