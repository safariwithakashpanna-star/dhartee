About Safari With Aakash

Safari With Aakash operates in the stunning and fragile tiger habitat of the Vindhya Hills in northern Madhya Pradesh, known for its dry deciduous forests, vast plateaus, deep gorges, and the pristine beauty of the Ken River. The website SafariWithAkash.com is founded by Akash Khangar, a dedicated wildlife tour guide with 10 years of experience exploring and interpreting this rich landscape. full list of services.

Flora & Fauna

Akash’s deep understanding of the region’s flora, fauna, and cultural heritage makes every safari educational, engaging, and truly memorable. As a passionate naturalist, he aims to offer travelers a safe, immersive, and responsible wildlife experience. With his expert guidance, visitors discover the true wilderness, hidden gems, and unique charm of Madhya Pradesh like never before.

Discover Panna National Park

Panna National Park is home to majestic Bengal tigers and diverse wildlife.
